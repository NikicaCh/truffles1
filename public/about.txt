ABOUT US
========
Truffles Macedonia is Europe's leading Lagotto Romagnolo breeder, specializing in champion bloodlines and truffle hunting dogs. With over a decade of dedicated breeding experience, we are recognized among Europe's most decorated FCI kennels.

OUR BREEDING PROGRAM
====================
- Health Testing: All breeding dogs are fully DNA-tested with certified hip and elbow X-rays
- Living Environment: Our dogs live freely in a 1,000 m² forest environment at 850 m altitude
- Socialization: Each puppy receives careful socialization, training, and personal attention
- Lifetime Support: We provide lifelong guidance on nutrition, training, and health care

ABOUT THE LAGOTTO ROMAGNOLO BREED
==================================
The Lagotto Romagnolo is an Italian water dog breed known for:
- Hypoallergenic curly coat (non-shedding)
- Exceptional truffle hunting abilities
- High intelligence and trainability
- Family-friendly temperament
- Active and energetic nature
- Loyalty and devotion
